Recipe Feud!

Game rules:

There are two players. A dish is randomly chosen. Each player has to take turns listing all the ingredients the chosen dish contains. The player who guesses the most ingredients within the their respective timer wins!

Hot tip! You can use CTRL + F to search for ingredients!
